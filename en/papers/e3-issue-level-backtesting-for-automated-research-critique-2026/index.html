Deep Analysis

Innovations

  • E3 automated review assistant that identifies decision-relevant technical concerns with nature, location, bearing, and resolution evidence
  • Issue-level backtesting protocol using post-cutoff papers and a meta-judge labeling issue-source pairs as Caught/Partial/Missed to avoid contamination

Methodology

E3 is evaluated on 100 ICLR 2026 papers using an issue-level backtesting protocol: a meta-judge (gpt-5.5) labels each of 4598 issue-source pairs as Caught, Partial, or Missed, comparing E3 against human reviews and two prompt-matched LLM baselines (gpt-5.4, claude-opus-4-6).

Key Results

E3 achieves the highest recall on all metrics: partial-inclusive recall 90.2% (15.5 points over GPT, 17.1 over Claude, 29.2 over human reviews), strict recall 65.8%; recovers 89.6% of human-raised concerns and surfaces 1635 additional issues missed by humans, 406 above the next-best source.
