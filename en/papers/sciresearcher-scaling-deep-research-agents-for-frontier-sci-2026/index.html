Deep Analysis

Innovations

  • Fully automated agentic framework for frontier-science data construction that synthesizes conceptual and computational tasks grounded in academic evidence.
  • Elicitation of information acquisition, tool-integrated reasoning, and long-horizon capabilities through curated data.
  • SciResearcher-8B, an agent foundation model that achieves state-of-the-art on HLE-Bio/Chem-Gold at its parameter scale, surpassing larger proprietary agents.

Methodology

SciResearcher is an automated agentic framework that constructs frontier-science data by synthesizing diverse tasks from sparse academic sources, then trains an 8B model via supervised fine-tuning and agentic reinforcement learning to perform information-seeking, tool-integrated reasoning, and long-horizon problem solving.

Key Results

SciResearcher-8B achieves 19.46% on HLE-Bio/Chem-Gold, setting a new state of the art at its scale and outperforming larger proprietary agents, with 13-15% absolute gains on SuperGPQA-Hard-Biology and TRQA-Literature.
